  • About LitterRoot

    LitterRoot is the landing page of the StinkLink Infrastructure

  • Mycelïm Interface

    The Mycelïm Interface is the method of organization for the StinkLink Infrastructure. Individual nodes, called hyphae, are the individual public domains that the StinkLink Mycelïm Interface spans across. Subdomains within each hypha that point to specific services are called Mushrims. An individual Mushrim is typically denoted by the prefix "Litter-", reflecting the connection to the StinkLink Mycelïm Interface.

  • Primary Hypha

    The single and predominant node is stinklink.net, wherefrom the StinkLink Mycelïm Interface derives its name. Additional hyphae will eventually be incorporated into the Interface, each having their own Mushrims. The eventual goal is a self-hosted, highly available, and redundant intranet with specific exposures to the internet.

  • Security

    All aspects of the StinkLink Mycelïm Interface are secured with various protocol to protect the core functions of the Mushrims, which provide utility to end-users based on their specific task designation. Currently, most security features are supported by Cloudflare's Zero Trust security infrastructure.
